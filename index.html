What is inspire?

We created an interactive environment for the Rock Hall that allows users
to collaborate and discover music based on their diverse musical preferences.

It started with
the idea

Using ideation techniques we set out to find a solution to the Rock n' Roll hall of fame's problem. They didn't have enough people coming back for multiple visits, how could we create an experience which resulted in people wanting to come to the Rock hall again and again? Our solution, a projection mapped structure.

Building the SPIre

We were able to translate our 3D renderings into a physical structure. Due to its exceptional size, we have broken our spire into three main pieces for ease of storage and transport.

The table UI

Through iteration we chose a shared, touch screen platform, to bring users together. The shared screen allows patrons to get close physically when interacting with the content together. The interactive nature of the screen allows them to feel that they are literally building the spire together.

The Technology

The development team has been key in creating a system that can sort and cross reference artists against each other. Currently, the developers have managed to create a network that takes user input and generates a meaningful shared artist on the many surfaces of the spire.
